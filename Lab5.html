Goal of the Course

As per the Syllabus, Goals of the Course Are:

  1. Understand basic concepts and technologies of data modeling in Relational Database Management System and Structured Query Language
  2. Understandthe basic technologies of Unstructured Database, Cloud Database, Data Warehouse, and Data Lake
  3. Be able to collect social media data or other online information from the Internet
